SAVE ALL KITTIES!

How to Play

Drunk Santa! You must save all the Kitties from the aggressive Wasps!
Pick up all the Kitties that are being swarmed by Wasps to gain points.
If the Wasps sting you, you will lose a life. Wasps only attack what's in front of them.
You have a gun to defend yourself, but it might not be easy to shoot a flying Wasp. Reloading will take a few seconds, so be wise with your shots.
And beware of not shooting any Kitties!
Eat Mushrooms to recover health, you have maximum of 3 lives!
